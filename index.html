The Experience

Hội An, without the crowd.

Cozy An Boutique Hotel sits on a quiet street in Cam Chau — close enough to walk the Old Town's lantern-lit lanes, far enough to come home to silence. Forty-eight rooms, a single courtyard pool, and a team who remember how you take your egg coffee by the second morning.

We built the hotel around the river, not around the room count. Every detail — the woven lanterns at the gate, the bicycles by the lobby, the breakfast cooked to order — is there because it belongs to Hội An, not because a brochure asked for it.
